Brief Intro

Created by Us, for Us

Nutty Buddies is a community built by people living with mental illness — not professionals, caregivers, or institutions.


We share what has actually helped us: grounding tools, crisis resources, personal stories, and small pieces of hope from people who understand what the struggle feels like from the inside.


Whether you’re in recovery, figuring things out, or just tired of being alone with it… you’re welcome here.
